# Digital Credential Issuance Support Guide
This page describes Service Levels and support details for the Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) [Digital Credential Issuance Platform (DCIP)](/README.md) provided to subscribing government agencies.

## Overview
The DCIP follows a layered support model. 
<p align="center"><img src="/assets/DCIP%20support%20overview.png" alt="DCIP support model" width="70%"/></p>



**MATTR → DPI and Status Page**  
MATTR provides technical support to DPI, including maintenance of the platform, escalation handling and resolution of bugs or performance issues.  

**DPI and Status Page → Subscriber Agency**  
The DPI Support Team provides onboarding assistance and operational support to the subscribing agency, and when required will escalate to MATTR. 

**Subscriber Agency → End Users**  
The subscribing agency is responsible for supporting their end users. This includes communication regarding impact to services.  
 

## Issuance Platform Service Commitments 

Digital Public Infrastructure have procured the [Enhanced Support](https://files.mattr.global/specsheets/v/mcc-support_2-1.pdf) service from MATTR which includes: 

* Platform availability per calendar month:  99.90% 
* 24/7 Support with named MATTR escalation contacts 
* Faster target response times
  
| Severity Level | First Response  | Subsequent Update |
|----------------|------------------|--------------------|
| LV. 1          | 1 Hour           | 2 hours            |
| LV. 2          | 2 Hours          | 4 hours            |
| LV. 3          | 1 Business Day   | 5 Business Days    |
| LV. 4          | 1 Business Day   | Best Efforts       |

| Business Continuity Category | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Recovery Point Objective (RPO) | 1 hour |
| Recovery Time Objective (RTO)  | 12 hours |

## Incident Process
In the event of DPI Issuance Platform outages or incidents, agencies should follow this triage process:

![DCIP Support Flow](/assets/DCIP%20support%20flow.png "DCIP incident support flow")

**MATTR Status Page** [https://status.nz01.mattr.global](https://status.nz01.mattr.global)

MATTR maintain a Status page for the MATTR VII Platform. Subscribing agencies must use this as their first point of triage in the event of issues with their credential issuing process and should subscribe to status notifications.

**DPI Support Email** [issuance@gdda.govt.nz](mailto:issuance@gdda.govt.nz)

During business hours, the Digital Public Infrastructure team will notify or can be notified of incidents via email.

**MATTR Support**

Subscribing agencies will be provided with an after-hours MATTR support phone number to be used **only** outside of business hours in the event of a Priority 1 event. This number is an answering service that will trigger the incident response process. Callers must clearly identify themselves and their contact details, and clearly indicate that the tenant is under the New Zealand Government shared agreement.
